# Sprint 6 Hero Shot List

## Purpose
Create a reusable capture list so the improved visuals immediately help the game's public-facing identity.

## Required shots
1. **Icon candidate**
   - close register foreground
   - player silhouette or hands near checkout
   - deep aisle or freezer tension in background
   - one strong title-space area left clear

2. **Thumbnail A — Closing shift tension**
   - wide checkout + aisle view
   - readable store branding
   - one uncanny clue in the distance

3. **Thumbnail B — Blackout moment**
   - same store identity, but with emergency-light mood
   - visual clarity preserved even in darkness

4. **Thumbnail C — Freezer dread**
   - cool-toned freezer area
   - strong silhouette and contrast
   - no overbusy composition

5. **Update / social shot**
   - before / after style reveal shot showing the move away from primitives

## Capture rules
- do not rely on UI for the composition
- avoid shots that could belong to any generic horror game
- foreground, midground, and background should all help sell "supermarket at 3AM"
- maintain the sprint art palette and signage language
